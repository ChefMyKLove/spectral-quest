Universe Foundation & Conceptual Framework

Spectral Quest: Rainbow Craft exists in a universe where color and emotion are literal forces woven by magical unicorns to sustain reality itself. The Spectral Coreâ€”source of all colorâ€”was shattered ten years ago by High Cleric Voss, an authoritarian zealot who views the full spectrum as "chaotic impurity" and seeks binary "perfection" (black/white/gray). Main Characters & Visual Design

  • Maxim/Maximum Voss (they/them, age 23): Lirien's cousin and former dance partner. Originally a silver-horned unicorn with dual-spectrum sight (could weave subtle pastels and deep shadows).   • The Seven Dancers (Chosen Family):

    • Veyra Prismwing (Violet, age 38): Architect with prismatic wings, teaches transcendence
  • High Cleric Voss (antagonist): Severe gray unicorn with broken horn, zealot obsessed with "purity"

Seven Rainbow Layers (Game Levels & Environments)

  1. Crimson Aether: Volcanic foundation, passionate reds, ember caverns. Rebel sanctuary. 7 Spectral Motes.
  2. Amber Layer: Time realm, clockwork mechanisms, hourglasses. Everything synced to cosmic heartbeat. 12 Motes.
  3. Yellow Realm: Eternal summer fields, laughing sunflowers, joy and hope. 18 Motes.
  4. Green Gardens: Wounded paradise, ghost-vines, Garden of First Light. Growth through grief. 24 Motes.
  5. Blue Depths: Underwater crystalline caverns, frozen tears, mirrors of truth. 35 Motes.
  6. Indigo Dreams: Between-space, flickering visions, prophecy realm. 44 Motes.
  7. Violet Crown: Highest frequency, Spectral Core location, Desaturation Engine. Final confrontation. 19 Motes + 7 Harmony Shards.

Total: 159 Spectral Motes across 7 levels. Each Mote collected mints a unique 1sat Ordinals NFT. Completing all Motes in a layer generates rare "Harmony Shard" NFT. Completing entire game creates ultimate "ORDINALRAINBOWS" master NFT.

Gameplay Mechanics & Web3 Integration

Browser-based game built with HTML5/JavaScript, accessible without downloads. Players control Lirien through point-and-click exploration, puzzle-solving (light-weaving patterns, rhythm matching, memory sequences), and non-violent conflict resolution (conversations, emotional connections, restoring color to desaturated areas). BSV blockchain integration enables:

  • Micropayments: Players earn real satoshi for completing levels (5-10 cents USD equivalent per level)
  • NFT Minting: Each Mote collected generates unique collectible; wallets optional (email-based onboarding for accessibility)
  • Ownership Model: Players truly "own" their journey through provable digital collectibles

Novel Component (30,000+ word novella)

Original fantasy prose expanding universe lore, focusing on:

  • The Shattering event (10 years prior): How Voss weaponized Spectral Core, Lirien/Maxim's childhood collaboration, the burn/exile
  • Maxim's decade of transformation: Body horror as metaphor for forced conformity, glimpses of consciousness trapped in machine
  • Seven Dancers backstories: How each became rebels, their relationships, teaching Lirien
  • Prophecy fragments: Cryptic verses woven throughout, revealing true nature of restoration
  • Emotional depth: Grief, chosen family, queer joy despite trauma, healing over revenge

Web Portal Architecture

Central hub website featuring:

  • Game Integration: Embedded playable experience or launcher
  • NFT Gallery: Display collected Motes/Shards, mint new ones, view ORDINALRAINBOWS rarity
  • Lore Codex: Character bios, layer descriptions, prophecy texts, world history
  • Community Features: Forums for players, beta tester feedback, links to queer gaming organizations
  • Novel Access: Digital download or web-reader for novella
  • Merchandise Shop: Prototypes displayed, future production info
