2. Project Description

Overview (500 words max):

This project creates original visual artworks establishing the aesthetic universe of "Spectral Quest: Rainbow Craft," a queer-led fantasy video game and novel series featuring Lirien, a non-binary unicorn weaver, and their journey to save their cousin Maxim while restoring seven shattered rainbow layers. As a Nanaimo-based queer visual artist, I will develop comprehensive concept art for characters, environments, and promotional materials that center 2SLGBTQI+ narratives and introduce a powerful new queer protagonist into the cultural lexicon.

The Story: Ten years ago, High Cleric Voss weaponized the Spectral Core against two young unicorn weavers‚ÄîLirien and their cousin Maxim‚Äîwhose collaborative rainbow-weaving threatened his vision of binary purity. Maxim's silver horn was severed, and over ten years of torture, they were transformed into a cyborg weapon powering the Desaturation Engine. Lirien, burned and exiled, was raised by seven rebel Dancers who each taught them crucial skills. Now 20 years old, Lirien must journey through seven fractured rainbow layers, collecting 159 Spectral Motes to restore the Harmony Shards and save Maxim from the machine that consumed them. This is a story about healing chosen family, not revenge‚Äîabout weaving light through darkness.

Visual Art Focus: This intensive 8-week project will produce standalone visual artworks including:

  • Character Designs (15 total): Lirien (non-binary protagonist with rainbow-weaving abilities), Maxim/Maximum (transformation from dancer to cyborg weapon), the Seven Dancers (Ember-crimson warrior, Calen-amber rhythm keeper, Sol-yellow healer, Sylvara-green growth weaver, Zorah-blue truth keeper, Nyx-indigo shadow dancer, Veyra-violet architect), High Cleric Voss (antagonist), supporting characters
  • Environment Concepts (7 layers): Each rainbow layer with distinct aesthetic‚ÄîCrimson Aether (passion/foundation), Amber Layer (time/rhythm), Yellow Realm (joy/hope), Green Gardens (growth/healing), Blue Depths (truth/clarity), Indigo Dreams (prophecy/vision), Violet Crown (transcendence/source)
  • Key Scenes (10 illustrations): The Shattering (Spectral Core breaking), The Seven Dancers mentoring Lirien, Lirien riding rainbow carpets, the Desaturation Engine, pivotal prophecy moments
  • Promotional Materials: 10 printable posters (various sizes), 5 social media template sets for marketing, cover art concepts for novel series
  • Merchandise Prototypes: Unicorn stuffie designs (Lirien and Maxim versions), action figure concepts, collectible art prints

Artistic Approach: The visual style blends traditional hand-sketching with digital illustration, emphasizing rainbow gradients, emotional depth through color theory, and non-binary aesthetics that reject rigid binaries. Using inks, paints, and digital tools (Photoshop, Procreate), I will create layered artworks exploring themes of identity, resilience, prophecy, and queer joy. Each of the seven rainbow layers will have distinct visual language tied to its thematic meaning and the Dancer who embodies it.

Community Impact: This project addresses severe underrepresentation of queer characters in gaming (only 2% feature LGBTQ+ leads, per GLAAD) and fantasy literature. By centering Lirien‚Äîa non-binary unicorn with they/them pronouns whose queerness is integral to their power‚Äîthis work introduces a new queer icon into popular culture. Visual outputs will engage 500+ community members through beta testing with QMUNITY, Generation Q, and VI Queer Collective, with Pride Month 2026 launch activations. 10% of eventual merchandise proceeds will support Vancouver Island LGBTQ2SIA+ youth programs.

Rationale for Visual Arts Fit: While this universe extends into game and novel formats (funded separately), this application isolates the visual art creation as standalone fine art practice‚Äîconcept art as contemporary illustration, promotional posters as graphic design, character sketches as character studies worthy of gallery exhibition. The work exists independently as visual storytelling that affirms queer identities through fantasy imagery.

11. Key Project Details Reference

Main Characters (Visual Art Focus):

  • Lirien Voss: 20-year-old non-binary protagonist (they/them). Evolved rainbow weaver who can breathe perfectly balanced rainbows and create complex constructions instantly. Wears rainbow-gradient flowing garments, has iridescent horn, rides self-created rainbow carpets.
  • Maxim Voss: Lirien's cousin, age 23. Originally a silver-horned unicorn with dual-spectrum sight (could weave subtle pastels and shadows). Visual transformation:
    • Before (age 13): Elegant silver horn, gentle eyes, dancing alongside Lirien
    • After (age 23/"Maximum"): Cyborg hybrid with golden-fused horn, one silver eye + one code-wheel eye, metallic hooves, haunted expression suggesting trapped consciousness
  • The Seven Dancers (mentors/chosen family):
    • Ember Coalheart (Crimson): Deep crimson coat darkening to black at hooves, ember-mane with heat shimmer, battle-scarred horn. Warrior-philosopher, age 47.
    • Calen Rhythmsoul (Amber): Warm amber coat, percussion cutie mark (drum with sound waves), horn etched with musical notation that glows. Age 32.
    • Sol Dawnwhisper (Yellow): Bright golden-yellow coat generating soft light, sunbeam mane, horn spiraled with gold leaf. Age 29.
    • Sylvara Voss (Green): Emerald coat shifting through forest shades, living vine mane with blooming flowers, moss-wrapped horn. Lirien's parent, age 41.
    • Zorah Truthkeeper (Blue): Deep ocean-blue with silver undertones, hourglass-shaped horn that ticks audibly, eyes seeing past/present/future. Age unknown (appears 50s).
    • Nyx Voidwalker (Indigo): Midnight-blue coat absorbing light, starlight mane, manifestable void wings, flickering horn. Maxim's former dance partner, Sylvara's partner. Age 35.
    • Veyra Prismwing (Violet): Pale violet coat with iridescent sheen, massive prismatic wings refracting rainbows, compound-faceted horn. Age 38.
  • High Cleric Voss: Antagonist. Severe, angular features, cold gray coat, broken/twisted horn symbolizing rejection of spectrum. Zealot obsessed with "purity" and binary order.

Seven Rainbow Layers (Environment Concepts):

  1. Crimson Aether: Foundation layer. Volcanic landscapes, passionate reds, ember-filled caverns. The rebel sanctuary location. Themes: passion, survival, grounding.
  2. Amber Layer: Time-themed realm. Clockwork mechanisms, hourglasses, suspended moments. Everything synced to cosmic heartbeat. Themes: rhythm, memory, temporal flow.
  3. Yellow Realm: Joy and light. Eternal summer fields, laughing sunflowers, warm breezes. Themes: hope, healing, optimism.
  4. Green Gardens: Growth and life. Once the Garden of First Light, now wounded but resilient. Ghost-vines reaching for absent sun. Themes: healing, growth through grief, chosen family.
  5. Blue Depths: Truth and clarity. Underwater crystalline caverns, frozen tears, mirrors reflecting reality. Themes: honesty, revelation, painful truths.
  6. Indigo Dreams: Between-space of prophecy. Flickering between visible/invisible, real/imagined. Half-formed visions. Themes: prophecy, dreams, potential futures.
  7. Violet Crown: Highest frequency, closest to Source. Where Spectral Core once pulsed. Now dominated by Desaturation Engine. Trembling, phase-shifting realm. Themes: transcendence, sacrifice, final confrontation.

Visual Motifs:

  • Rainbow Carpets: Lirien's signature magic‚Äîwoven light paths they ride through the Aether
  • Spectral Motes: Glowing fragments (7 red, 12 orange, 18 yellow, 24 green, 35 blue, 44 indigo, 49 violet) scattered across layers
  • Binary Wrap: Visualized as grayscale corruption spreading like digital static, freezing color into black/white/gray
  • Desaturation Engine: Massive cyborg-organic machine pulsing beneath Violet Crown, tubes connecting to Maxim's body
  • Prophecy Symbols: Seven-pointed star (rainbow layers), shattered prism (broken Spectral Core), weaver's horn motif
