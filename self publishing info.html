SPECTRAL QUEST: RAINBOW CRAFT

Before you learned to see, you learned to dream in colour! Yes, even from deep inside that dark and exuberantly riotous womb-space, you learned what the colours meant as they moved and fused to become the reality all around us, inside and out. Knowledge so innate that it BECAME you.

You didn't even ever question the way that some shades of red made your heart race, or the way that a certain hue of blue could break it. The subtle way that yellow tastes like summer, or how violet hums like a lullaby just before sleep, are sensations ingrained into your being from the very moment you became. And through it all, after all that you've been taught about it, you thought colour was physics: wavelengths showering the electromagnetic illusion of light onto the canvas of our eyes.

But you weren't taught about the Unicorns. You weren't taught that colour IS the fabric that holds together every breath you take. What you were taught about unicorns AND reality was wrong, or at least missing some crucial information: For example, did you know that somewhere beyond the veil of what you here in the third dimension call reality, magical herds of Unicorns are working with every fibre of their life force... just to keep you dreaming AND breathing?
